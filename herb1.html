Lavender

Lavender is very attractive to bees because of its color. Bumblebees and Honeybees are the most attracted insects for lavender. Bees are not discriminatory when it comes to the type or color variation of lavender. However, since the L. Angustifolia varieties either bloom continuously or multiple times, they are the most beneficial for the bees to have a consistent source of lavender pollen.
