Cilantro

Cilantro also is known as coriander and is not only a very popular herb in the kitchen but with Honeybees as well. Cilantro is an annual herb which means it only grows once a year but provides a healthy amount of nectar and pollen for bees. The smell of cilantro also repels mites that prey on bees, so they use the herb to rid themselves.
