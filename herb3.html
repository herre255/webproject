Thyme

Thyme comes in a variety of different variation all of which are popular with bees. Thyme is attractive to not only honeybees but with sweat bees, hoverflies, and wasps. Not only is Thyme useful for bees to pollinate from but it can be used frequently in the kitchen. It is also a perennial herb which means it lives for more than two years.
